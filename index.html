About Swans

1. Greed (1986)

While many fans of the band Swans would not set this album above the bands 2014 record "To Be Kind",
it sits as my number one due to my love for the bleak and hopeless emotions the album presents in context
with the 1980s NYC scene the album was created in.

2. To be Kind (2014)

To be Kind, the album a lot of people will put as their #1. This album is a monster of a Rock composition
with every musical corner of Swans packed into one with a runtime of just 2 hours. Even with how highly
acclaimed this album is within the bands discography, it is still not for the casual music listener as this project
has a lot to unpack.

3. White Light from the Mouth of Infinity (1981)

This is one of the groups earlier projects that would follow their 1987 sophmore album "Children of God".
This was at the time where the group was still following heavy and rhythmic roots and more traditional song
writing that brought them to where they are today.
